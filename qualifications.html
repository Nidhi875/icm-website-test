Qualifications in the United Kingdom

Gouldings Global Academy delivers internationally recognised qualifications designed to prepare learners for higher education, employment and professional development across a wide range of academic and vocational disciplines.

Our qualifications are developed using internationally recognised academic standards and quality assurance processes, providing learners with clear progression pathways into higher education and employment.

The Qualifications Framework

The Gouldings Global Academy Qualifications Framework provides a structured pathway that enables learners to progress from introductory qualifications through to advanced academic and professional awards.

Each qualification level has clearly defined learning outcomes, academic standards and progression routes, ensuring consistency, quality assurance and international recognition across all programmes.

Level Descriptor

Qualification levels define the knowledge, understanding, practical skills, responsibility and autonomy expected from learners at each stage of study.

As learners progress through the levels they develop increasingly advanced analytical, technical and professional competencies, preparing them for higher education and employment opportunities.

Total Qualification Time (TQT)

Every Gouldings Global Academy qualification is assigned a Total Qualification Time (TQT). TQT represents the estimated number of hours that a typical learner is expected to spend achieving the learning outcomes of the qualification.

Total Qualification Time includes guided learning, independent study, assessment preparation, assignments and examinations.

Qualification titles are determined according to Total Qualification Time.

  • Award – up to 120 Total Qualification Hours
  • Certificate – 121–369 Total Qualification Hours
  • Diploma – 370+ Total Qualification Hours
